Dr. Norman Borlaug

The man who saved a billion lives

Dr. Norman Borlaug, American agronomist, humanitarian, and Nobel laureate.

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist, humanitarian, and Nobel laureate who has been called "the father of the Green Revolution". He is credited with saving over a billion people worldwide from starvation. Borlaug was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize, the Presidential Medal of Freedom, and the Congressional Gold Medal for his work.
